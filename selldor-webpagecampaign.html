whilst on the road!

SellDOR is a multiple-purpose Native Mobile APP, designed to solve the challenges that each trading & distribution company faces when dealing with traditional devices.

MAIN FEATURES

Salesman Routing

Mainly dedicated to distribution companies, the system guides the sales representatives or the cash van drivers on the daily itineraries drawn by sequence of scheduled destinations to cover.

 

Location identification/Time Attendance/Check in-out

At each visit, a Check In and Check Out permits to trace the salesman at his time of arrival and departure from a customer or lead. It also captures his location at the time of checking in, then checkout, and matches it with the clients GPS coordination.

 

Products’ Catalog

Easy and fast ways to search for items with filters by code, barcode, family, brand or description; next to each item a picture makes of the SellDor mobile APP the best catalogue for displaying the goods of the company, Online Quantities, Discounts, Free quantities, clients’deal and Promotions.

Sales Tracking with Sales Order Approval

 

Cash Van Operation

Friendly and fast maneuvering on the tablet’s touch screen for entering Sales order while on the field, and having it instantly saved on the company’s servers, leading to fast processing of the order in the back office. Invoices, Sales Order, Receipt with various modes of payments, Sales Return or Request of returns may be printed on Wi-Fi/Bluetooth printers; these may be mailed to the client on the spot.

 

Salesman Visit Reports

Sales notes and time sheet may be entered where remarks are immediately communicated to the concerned managers.

Dolphin BI & Dashboards

Summarized dashboards related to closing day, Global sales reports, statistics by customer, Monthly sales reports, KPIs, …

 

Clients Management

Get full view of your contacts, address, phone numbers, conversation history, statuses (idle, leads and customers) …Save also their GPS coordinates.

 

Accounts Management

Credit limit, Clients balance, Statement of account, Outstanding Invoices, sales statistics, orders based on the Workflow …

 

Contacts Management

Centralize your data by keeping contact persons, their personal info, individual emails professional and personals, mobile numbers… all in one place, accessible in one click.

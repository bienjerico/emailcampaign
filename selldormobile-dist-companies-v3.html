Improve your distribution process, increase your sales performance, speed up your ordering flow... whilst on the road!

 

SellDOR is a multiple-purpose Native Mobile APP, designed to solve the challenges that each trading & distribution company faces when dealing with traditional devices.

 

It combines in a single mobile computing software, real time access to up-to-date client's information, pre-sales, orders taking, cashvan operations, daily routing schedule, sales team location tracking (by time), client collections, product catalogue, product pricing, promotions, Inventory and much more...

 

 

 

SellDOR is fully compliant with VAT regulations within the GCC.

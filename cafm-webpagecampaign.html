Computer Aided Facilities Management

Visual Dolphin Computer Aided Facilities Management to reduce your management costs & time

Automate all the processes of your Facilities and manage all Maintenance activities, from first customer call all the way to execution then billing & payment.
 

Preventive Maintenance and Schedules

Preventive Maintenance and Schedule module is multi-faceted system which ensures that all equipment’s receives consistent maintenance. You can plan and schedule periodical maintenance well in advance and track the status of each schedule by equipment which helps to improve overall efficiency of maintenance activity.

  • Create a centralized Schedule of preventative maintenance works
  • Track list of PM works from the total number of due in each month.
  • Track all status of the PM works assigned for each technicians.
  • Track material, labor, and other costs incurred for each PM works.
  • Track the full history of the PM works for each asset/equipment’s
  • Easily drag and drop with calendar schedule.
 
 

Tickets and Work Orders

Ticketing system will allow you to generate tickets based on the maintenance complaints and will process through workflow to generate work orders. It will allow you to record all necessary detail of complaints like problem type, problem description, location and uploading picture and files.

  • Receiving complaints and filling a Ticket Request.
  • Forwarding the ticket to the Maintenance Dept (Workflow) and generating work order.
  • Assigning Tasks to persons for inspecting, fixing & repairing works
  • Easily update Work Order status through workflow
  • Track the full history of each work order from the time it’s created to closing the job
  • Track material, labor, and other costs
  • Auto-generates email notification.
  • Monitoring the status of each work order and generating reports.
 
 

Inventory Control

Inventory Module allows you to itemize the spare parts and other consumables required for the daily usage. You can enter part details such as model numbers, descriptions, unit costs, suppliers, etc. System allows users to receive automated alerts when any items reach minimum stock levels. This module helps you run your store with efficiency having many features such as supplier contact details, lead time on deliveries, min/optimum values and reorder points.

  • Manage all supplier reference details for each inventory item
  • Track all the transactions related to purchases, issuance and return of items in store
  • Alerts on reorder points
  • Replenish stocks based on the minimum/optimum level
  • Purchase order workflow and online approval.
  • Comprehensive inventory reports
 
 

Procurement

With Procurement system, all the purchase requisitions will be centralized and monitored efficiently with status of each PR. Users can process the purchase requisitions against work orders or for an expenses. Procurement department can issue the RFQ to the different suppliers, Compare the prices and finally sent to Management for approval electronically.

  • Manage Supplier Contact details
  • Defining Workflow for Procurement
  • Enquiry of list of PR and Monitoring status of each PR/PO
  • Sending RFQ to different suppliers, price comparison Sheet and document attachment.
  • Approving best priced RFQ and canceling other.
  • Sending LPO to supplier by mail
  • Procurement for Job or Financial Mode
 
 

Assets Management

With Asset Module, you can maintain complete list of assets, with hierarchy of assets category, movements and Depreciation history.

  • Keep track of detailed information on all your assets.
  • Import all assets information in one go from excel or csv format.
  • Tracking Asset by location, by employee, by cluster, by building
  • History of all purchase related information on all assets
  • Generate and print asset barcode Labels within the system.
  • Attach images, documents and manuals for each asset.
 
 

Dashboards and Reports

The Dashboard module helps to build and visualize custom made KPIs to enhance the performance of each service line departments. It comes with various built in standard dashboard such as Statistics on repairs and fixes, aging of tickets, analysis by problem types etc.

  • Automatically updates the dashboard reports.
  • Allows you to create many dashboard templates
  • Creates customized dashboard
  • Security on dashboard.
 
 

Mobile App

As soon a task is assigned & scheduled, it may send a notification to the concerned person through Job DOR mobile app. From a mobile, the team member may review his tasks, start a new one, write notes, enter his time sheet, and schedule his client visits accordingly.

  • Notification when assigning a task to the technician
  • View all task assign to the concerned persons
  • Enter timesheet on a specific task
  • Check in /check out
